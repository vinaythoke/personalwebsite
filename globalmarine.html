Global Marine Boats

Professional Website

This is the website I have crafted for the Global Marine Boats. This design is fully responsive and will work on desktop, tablets, and any other mobile devices or small screen devices. I started with simple sketching, wireframing and lots of brainstorming all the way to visual design to communicate the desired message effectively to its intended audience. Clean, uncluttered, simple yet effective and feature-rich design with lots of prominent calls to action to help the user take action without being distracted. Things required to be communicated: Client's ability to give an immediate fair amount of cash to liquidate boats, An extensive inventory of boats to sell, they are highly connected in the yacht market and can make any deal a visitor wants to happen. Thank you for having look at the project. Feel free to get in touch if you are looking to enhance your business using the power of visual design communication.
